Vivian's Story

This is who we are.

Vivian has over 2 decades of experience with hair. Graham Webb Academy trained, she has worked with PR Partners, Bubbles Salon, Nash Hair Design in Falls Church City (she goes by Lin), and has now ventured out on her own to open this gorgeous salon in Vienna in the heart of the historic town. Vivian is known for saying "Hair color evolves. It reflects the life you're living, where you're traveling, what you're eating... and to keep up with the pace of your life, I add dimension and texture to enhance what is naturally occurring." Vivian is an expert at balayage, color corrections, and formulating the perfect concoctions to achieve multi-dimensional results!
